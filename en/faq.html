FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions about Transportation Services

1. General Information

All transportation services are provided by GroundK, the official transportation partner for this event. Services include airport transfers, cargo/bike transport, and vehicle rentals.

The event is held at Mona Yongpyong Resort, Pyeongchang, Gangwon Province, Korea. Travel distances:

  • From Incheon International Airport (ICN): approximately 2.5–3 hours by vehicle
  • From Gimpo International Airport (GMP): approximately 2–2.5 hours by vehicle

Yes, but GroundK is the recommended and coordinated service provider for this event. They are experienced with the UCI event logistics and can handle bikes, cargo, and large team movements efficiently.

2. Airport Transfer Services

GroundK provides transfers from both Incheon International Airport (ICN) and Gimpo International Airport (GMP) to Yongpyong Resort, and return trips.

Yes. All airport transfer vehicles — minivans, minibuses, coach buses, and cargo/freight trucks — are operated with a professional driver included. There are no self-drive options for airport transfers.

Please provide the following when requesting a quotation:

  • Arrival and departure dates, times, and flight numbers
  • Number of passengers
  • Number of suitcases / bags
  • Number of bicycle boxes or cases
  • Any special cargo or equipment

Yes, and in fact this is required. Bicycles and large cargo cannot be loaded into minivans or minibuses — they must travel on a separate cargo truck. GroundK will typically recommend a combined solution (e.g., minibus for passengers + cargo truck for bikes).

Yes. Quotations can cover one-way or round-trip transfers between the airport and resort.

3. Bicycle & Cargo Transport

No. Bicycle boxes and bike cases cannot be loaded into minivans, minibuses, or coach buses. Bikes must be transported separately on a dedicated cargo truck (1-ton, 2.5-ton, or 5-ton, depending on volume).

GroundK offers the following freight truck options:

  • 1-ton truck – suitable for smaller loads (a few bikes + bags)
  • 2.5-ton truck – medium capacity
  • 5-ton truck – for large teams with many bicycle boxes and suitcases
A 5-ton truck is generally recommended for teams with 10+ bicycle boxes and 20+ pieces of luggage.

Yes. All cargo/freight trucks are operated with a driver. They are not available as self-drive vehicles.

No special permits are required. GroundK's drivers handle all logistics on your behalf.

No. In Korea, cargo trucks cannot be rented without a driver. All cargo truck services must be booked with a professional driver included.

Capacity varies by box size and packing, but a 1-ton truck can typically accommodate around 8–12 bicycle boxes plus associated luggage. If you have more than 12 bikes, a 2.5-ton or 5-ton truck is recommended. Contact GroundK directly to confirm capacity for your specific setup.

Yes. If your team is staying outside the Yongpyong Resort venue and needs to transport bikes to/from the race site on a daily basis, GroundK can arrange cargo truck service for the race week period as well. Please inquire when submitting your quotation.

4. Vehicle Rental (Self-Drive)

Yes. GroundK offers self-drive rental cars (no driver) for use during your stay. Pickup is available at Incheon International Airport (ICN) or Yongpyong Resort.

Depending on the vehicle, you may be able to fold seats and fit 1–2 boxed bicycles, but availability cannot be guaranteed. We recommend using a cargo truck for safe and reliable transport.

There is no stated minimum, but rentals are typically arranged on a per-day basis. Most teams rent for the duration of the event week.

Rental car pickup is available at Incheon International Airport (ICN) or Yongpyong Resort.

GroundK also offers a charter vehicle with driver, which is a full-day service for teams that prefer a dedicated driver rather than self-driving. Please contact GroundK for pricing details.

5. Payment Methods

GroundK accepts the following payment methods:

  • PayPal
  • Credit card
  • Bank transfer (wire transfer)

Payment details and timing are confirmed upon quotation acceptance. Please ask GroundK for specific payment terms when you receive your quotation.

6. How to Request a Quotation

There are two ways:

  1. Online Form: Visit the transportation page and fill in the inquiry form with your requirements.
  2. Email: Send your details directly to hospitality@groundk.com

To receive an accurate quote, please include:

  • Your team name and contact information
  • Arrival airport (ICN or GMP), date, time, and flight number
  • Departure airport, date, time, and flight number
  • Number of passengers
  • Number of suitcases / pieces of luggage
  • Number of bicycle boxes or cases
  • Whether you need rental vehicles during the event week (and how many)
  • Any special cargo or requirements

GroundK typically responds within 1–2 business days. For complex requirements, they may need additional time to prepare the document.
